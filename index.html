About Me

I'm Marek from Poland, I'm 21. I always dreamed about creating my own songs and to share my feelings with others by music. Now I'm about to realize this dream :) I started producing few months ago, when I listened to Alan Walker's Q&A. For me, music is about telling stories by words, melody, and rhythm, it's sphere when you can share your personality with other people who are similar. I hope you will enjoy my music, and also thanks for every comments, likes and subs, it really matters. Greetings from Poland.Listen to my songs and playlists and imagine good moments of your cool life.
